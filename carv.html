Carv

Carv is a digital ski coach that uses pressure sensing and 9-axis motion to detect technique, terain, and ability.

I was in one of Carv's graduate positions as a Hardware Intern. I worked on the design, testing, and manufacturing of their product. I lead the testing of their next generation to validate it for mass production and created algoritms to diagnose hardware issues in the product from our database using Python which were implemented in our web app to reduce warranties.
